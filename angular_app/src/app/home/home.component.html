What this website is about

This website is part personal blog, part laboratory. It's a space where I can dive deep into the juicy details of topics that spark my curiosity. Each post is the refined result of months of research, programming and animation, distilled into something that is hopefully as fun to read as it was to create. This website will likely get updated on a monthly basis, as soon as I finish anything sufficiently cool to talk about.

Philosophy behind the website

I'm a firm believer that one of the best ways to learn is through curiosity and interaction, regardless of the topic. For me there's nothing more fun than learning by doing, experimenting, and breaking things on the way.

However, real understanding only comes when trying to explain to others, and for the purpose of this website, it comes down to two things: visualization and writing.

I decided to invest in visualization and interactive demonstrations because they can be invaluable explanation tools. A clever visual can turn confusion into insight, and a bit of interactivity can turn a dry formula into something you feel rather than just memorize.

Writing, on the other hand, helps me structure my thoughts, organize messy ideas, and break them down to their essence. In a way, it forces me to learn things twice: once by doing, and once by explaining.

Hence, I didn't write this website out of altruistic intentions alone. It's also an active effort to deepen my knowledge and knowledge-sharing abilities.
